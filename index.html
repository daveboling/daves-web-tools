Dave's Web Dev Tools and Tutorials

Hello and welcome to this Github page and/or website.

The goal of my web dev tools is to teach very very new developers modern front-end web development skills by example.

So, with that being said. If I feel like something is too complex or mysterious, I will comment the tutorial right next to the code. That's right, no pretty drafted-up typography with a nicely designed page. (Sorry designers)

In that last paragraph, you'll notice an emphasis on mysterious. Let me first state that I am also a newbie programmer, you may find many mistakes that are not considered semantic or modern, but they accomplish the same thing. Keep that dully noted. With that being said, in programming a lot of things just 'work'. As a person with a background in C++ and programming in general, mysteries drive me NUTS. 80% of my time is spent understanding code, the other 20% is writing. I want you to understand too.

Henceforth...

...the catchphrase 'No mysteries!' will be drilled in your head!

Let's get to it y'all.
